Automatically share your Telegram posts on Wordpress

== Description ==
@publishareBot is a bot which automatically publish your Telegram posts to other platforms like your blog, your website, Twitter...

This plugin gives you a web endpoint out-of-the-box to interface your WP website with the bot.

== Installation ==
Once installed, the plugin will automatically generate an endpoint URL to be used with the Telegram bot @publishareBot.
You just have to copy the link and paste it to the bot settings.

By uninstalling the plugin, the endpoint will stop working.

To get more info about the service and the project, just visit the official website (https://publishare.0x100.it).
